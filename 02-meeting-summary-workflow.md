# AI Meeting Summary Workflow

## Problem

Project managers frequently need to convert meeting discussions into structured meeting minutes.

## AI Solution

Generative AI tools can analyze meeting notes and produce structured summaries automatically.

## Example Prompt

Act as a Technical Project Manager.

Summarize the following meeting notes and provide:

• Key Decisions  
• Action Items  
• Responsible Team  
• Potential Project Risks

## Benefits

Using AI for meeting summaries can:

• reduce manual documentation time  
• improve consistency of meeting records  
• ensure important decisions are clearly captured
