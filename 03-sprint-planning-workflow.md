# AI-Assisted Sprint Planning

## Problem

Sprint planning sessions require reviewing backlog items and organizing tasks into achievable sprint goals.

## AI Solution

AI tools can help project managers analyze backlog items and suggest an initial sprint plan.

## Example Prompt

Act as an Agile coach.

Based on the following backlog items, propose a 2-week sprint plan for a team of 6 developers.

Include:

• task priority  
• estimated effort  
• possible dependencies

## Benefits

AI-assisted sprint planning can:

• speed up planning preparation  
• help identify dependencies  
• provide a starting point for team discussion
